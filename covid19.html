2021 Festivities Altered Due To COVID-19

After initially canceling all festivities, we have decided to hold the fireworks show. The display will take place on Friday, July 2nd with spectating from the Bristol Rec Fields. The fireworks will begin at dusk, likely around 9:45pm - show up early! Vendors will be on hand and parking will be provided by the Masons.

Additionally, we will host our annual chicken BBQ fundraiser on Wednesday, June 23. Visit the Chicken BBQ page for more information and thank you in advance for your support!

See you soon!

Bristol Fourth of July Committee
